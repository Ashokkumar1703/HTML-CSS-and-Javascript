Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is known for his revolutionary writings. His poems often dealt with the social and political issues of his time, and his revolutionary spirit helped him become a national icon.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul was a prolific writer who wrote in various genres, including poetry, prose, and drama. He is particularly known for his ghazals and songs, many of which have become popular throughout the Bengali-speaking world.

Awards

  • Jagattariel Gold Medal
  • Padma Bhushan
  • National Poet of Bangladesh
  • Ekushey Padak

Poetry

Some of Nazrul's most famous poems include:

  • Bidrohi
  • Prabhat
  • Agnikankana

Poems and Songs

Nazrul wrote over 4,000 songs, many of which have been recorded and are still popular today.
